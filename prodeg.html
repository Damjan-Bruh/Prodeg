Enemy Shooter
In this game, you are a gun that is pointing in the sky. But, randomly, some humans pop out and you need to shoot them. But you need to be fast, because your time is ticking and if you are not fast enough, you lose. Controls are: a and d for left and right, left arrow and right arrow for faster left and right and s is for shooting.

Things needed for .py file:

  • Python 3.12
  • Installed libraries: pygame
  • Copy of Enemy Shooter

Laser Atack
In this game, you are a gun in space, that shoots in four directions(up, down, left, right). You need to shoot the bullets that are comming. They are comming out of all four directions and each direction has three bullets, but you also have three bullets for each direction. Controls are: w, a, s, d for left, right, up and down, t, f, g, h for left, right, up and down and i, j, k, l for left, right, up and down.

Things needed for .py file:

  • Python 3.12
  • Installed libraries: pygame
  • Copy of Laser Atack
